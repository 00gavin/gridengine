London e-Science Centre (LeSC) at Imperial College London announces Globus Toolkit 3 integration with Grid Engine

September 19, 2003

The London e-Science Centre at Imperial College London is pleased to announce an integration of an open source scheduling environment Sun Grid Engine (Standard and Enterprise Edition) into GT3 which builds on our previous GT2 integration. Further details and the installation instuctions may be found here -

http://www.lesc.ic.ac.uk/projects/epic-gt-sge.html
Any comments and corrections should be sent to:
GT2 - marko.krznaric@imperial.ac.uk
GT3 - david.mcbride@imperial.ac.uk
This work has been supported through a collaborative project - EPIC - between Sun Microsystems and the Department of Trade and Industry as part of the UK's e-Science Core programme.
