Web Services and SGE - Open DRMAA Service Provider (OpenDSP )

    By Piotr Domagalski, PSNC

As a Sun Center of Excellence, Poznan Supercomputing and Networking Center (PSNC) has been actively participating in grid environment research and development. This presentation will focus on development of the Open DRMAA Service Provider (OpenDSP ) that provides remote access to Distributed Resource Management (DRM) systems, including SGE. We present key features of the service - support for widely adopted standards (OGF, OASIS, OGSA), flexible authentication, authorization and accounting mechanisms, high performance in comparison to Globus GRAM and integration with external services (WS-Notification Broker, Identity Provider, PayPal ). We also mention the formation of the FedStage open source initiative that aims to support these products and technologies in the market.
